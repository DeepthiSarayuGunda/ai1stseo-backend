# Free Social Posting Automation Tools — Multi-Platform Comparison

Research date: April 2026

## Tool Profiles

### 1. Buffer
- **Free plan:** Yes
- **Platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube, Threads, Bluesky, Mastodon, Google Business
- **Free limits:** 3 channels, 10 scheduled posts per channel at a time (slot reopens after publish)
- **Automation:** Basic scheduling, best-time suggestions
- **API:** Yes (publish API available on paid plans)
- **Limitations:** No analytics on free, no team features, 10-post queue cap per channel
- **Paid starts at:** $6/channel/month (Essentials)

### 2. Zoho Social
- **Free plan:** Yes
- **Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Google Business Profile
- **Free limits:** 1 brand, up to 6 channels (1 per network), 7 posts per channel/week
- **Automation:** Scheduling, SmartQ optimal timing
- **API:** Yes (via Zoho ecosystem)
- **Limitations:** No bulk scheduling on free, limited analytics (basic), no team collaboration
- **Paid starts at:** $15/month (Standard)

### 3. Metricool
- **Free plan:** Yes
- **Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, Google Business, Threads, Bluesky, Mastodon (no LinkedIn or X/Twitter on free)
- **Free limits:** 1 brand, 50 scheduled posts/month, 5 competitor profiles, 3 months analytics history
- **Automation:** Scheduling, auto-lists, best-time posting
- **API:** No public API on free
- **Limitations:** No LinkedIn or X/Twitter on free plan, 50 post/month cap, no AI features
- **Paid starts at:** $22/month (Starter, adds LinkedIn + X for $5/mo add-on)

### 4. Publer
- **Free plan:** Yes
- **Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube, Google Business, Mastodon (no X/Twitter on free)
- **Free limits:** 3 social accounts, unlimited scheduling
- **Automation:** Scheduling, auto-scheduling, bulk scheduling, RSS feeds (limited)
- **API:** No public API on free
- **Limitations:** No X/Twitter on free (Enterprise API cost), no AI features, limited analytics
- **Paid starts at:** $12/month (Professional, adds X/Twitter)

### 5. Later
- **Free plan:** Limited (very restricted)
- **Platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest
- **Free limits:** 1 social set, 5-10 posts per profile/month, 5 AI credits/month
- **Automation:** Visual planner, basic scheduling
- **API:** No public API
- **Limitations:** Very low post limits, Instagram-focused, minimal analytics, no bulk scheduling
- **Paid starts at:** $25/month (Starter)

### 6. SocialBee
- **Free plan:** No (14-day free trial only)
- **Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube, Google Business, Threads, Bluesky
- **Free limits:** N/A (trial: 5 profiles, 1 workspace)
- **Automation:** Category-based scheduling, content recycling, RSS automation, AI assistant
- **API:** No public API
- **Limitations:** No free tier — starts at $29/month after trial
- **Paid starts at:** $29/month (Bootstrap)

### 7. Hootsuite
- **Free plan:** No (discontinued in 2023)
- **Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube
- **Free limits:** N/A
- **Automation:** Bulk scheduling, auto-scheduling, social inbox, listening
- **API:** Yes (on paid plans)
- **Limitations:** No free plan at all, starts at $99/month — priced for teams/enterprise
- **Paid starts at:** $99/month (Professional)

### 8. Postiz (Open Source)
- **Free plan:** Yes (self-hosted, fully free)
- **Platforms:** Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, Reddit, Threads, Bluesky, Mastodon, Telegram, Discord, Slack + more (18+ channels)
- **Free limits:** Unlimited (self-hosted)
- **Automation:** Scheduling, cross-posting, first-comment automation, AI agent, webhooks, repeated posts
- **API:** Yes (open source, full access)
- **Limitations:** Requires self-hosting (Docker), technical setup needed, you manage infrastructure
- **Paid starts at:** Free forever (self-hosted) or hosted plans available

### Why Buffer Wins

1. **Platform coverage:** Buffer's free plan is the only one that includes LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Facebook, AND Instagram without restrictions. Publer and Metricool both exclude X/Twitter from their free tiers.

2. **Simplicity:** No self-hosting, no technical setup. Sign up, connect 3 channels, start posting.

3. **Queue model:** The 10-post queue per channel is a rolling limit — once a post publishes, the slot opens. For 2-3 posts/day, this is sufficient.

4. **Growth path:** When ready to scale, Buffer's per-channel pricing ($6/channel) is among the cheapest paid options.

### When to Choose Alternatives

- **Zoho Social** — if you're already in the Zoho ecosystem (CRM, Desk) and want native integration
- **Publer** — if you need unlimited scheduling and can live without X/Twitter initially
- **Postiz** — if you have technical capacity to self-host and want zero platform restrictions with full API access
- **Metricool** — if analytics and competitor tracking matter more than LinkedIn/X posting

### What's Missing in Every Free Tier

- No bulk scheduling (except Publer)
- No content recycling / evergreen reposting
- No AI-powered caption generation
- No team collaboration or approval workflows
- No advanced analytics or reporting
- No API access for programmatic posting (except Postiz self-hosted)
