Understanding Automation Jobs
These are background tasks that run automatically to keep your data fresh. A green dot means the job completed successfully on its last run. Amber means it's pending or had a warning. Grey means it hasn't run yet. More frequent runs = more data points = more accurate trend analysis. The total runs counter shows how much historical data has been collected.
