🚨 Understanding Alerts
The Alert Engine automatically scans your probe data for threats. Critical alerts mean your GEO score dropped significantly — investigate immediately. High alerts flag competitors overtaking you on specific keywords. Medium alerts indicate declining confidence — AI models are less certain about your brand. Each alert includes a recommended action.
Tiered Keyword Monitoring Schedule
📡 Understanding Tiered Monitoring
Keywords are monitored at different frequencies based on their commercial value. Tier 1 (highest value) is checked every 2 days using Nova Lite for speed. Tier 2 runs weekly. Tier 3 (research keywords) runs bi-weekly using Ollama to save costs. This ensures your most important keywords get the freshest data.
Controlled Experiments
🧪 Understanding Experiments
These are A/B tests for your content structure. Each experiment holds a keyword constant and varies the content format (e.g., FAQ vs paragraph, list vs table). After 4 weeks, we measure which format gets more AI citations. Ollama is used for exploratory tests (free), Nova Lite for production validation.
