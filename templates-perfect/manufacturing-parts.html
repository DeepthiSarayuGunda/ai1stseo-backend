About NovaTech Industries

NovaTech Industries is defined as a precision manufacturing company specializing in CNC machined components for high-reliability applications. Founded in 2008 in Mississauga, Ontario, the company operates a 45,000 square foot facility equipped with 28 CNC machines including 5-axis milling centers and Swiss-type lathes.

Our team of 120 employees includes 15 certified manufacturing engineers and 8 quality inspectors. According to data from the Canadian Manufacturers and Exporters Association updated in 2025, NovaTech ranks among the top 50 precision machining companies in Ontario by revenue. We process over 2 million parts annually with a first-pass yield rate of 99.4%.

We manufacture custom CNC machined parts to customer specifications. Every part is produced from certified raw materials with full traceability documentation.

Manufacturing Process

Our manufacturing process follows a structured workflow designed to ensure quality and on-time delivery at every stage.

  1. Design Review. Our engineering team reviews your CAD files and identifies potential manufacturability issues before production begins.
  2. Material Procurement. We source certified raw materials from approved suppliers. All materials include mill certificates and full traceability.
  3. CNC Programming. Our programmers create optimized toolpaths using Mastercam and Siemens NX software to minimize cycle time and maximize accuracy.
  4. Production. Parts are machined on our 28 CNC machines with in-process inspection at critical dimensions.
  5. Quality Inspection. Every part is inspected using coordinate measuring machines with accuracy to 0.0001 inches. First article inspection reports are provided for all new parts.
  6. Packaging and Shipping. Parts are cleaned, packaged to prevent damage, and shipped via your preferred carrier with tracking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our customers. For specific project inquiries, contact our engineering team at engineering@novatechindustries.com.

What materials does NovaTech machine?
NovaTech machines aluminum 6061 and 7075, stainless steel 304 and 316, titanium Grade 5, brass, copper, Delrin, PEEK, and carbon steel. We maintain certified material traceability for all aerospace and medical grade materials.
What tolerances can NovaTech achieve?
Our 5-axis CNC machines achieve tolerances of plus or minus 0.001 inches for standard parts and plus or minus 0.0005 inches for precision aerospace components. All parts are verified using CMM inspection.
What is the typical lead time for custom parts?
Standard lead time is 2 to 3 weeks for production runs. We offer expedited 5-day turnaround for prototype and urgent orders. Lead times vary based on complexity, quantity, and material availability.
Is NovaTech ISO certified?
Yes, NovaTech Industries holds ISO 9001:2015 certification and AS9100D certification for aerospace manufacturing. We undergo annual third-party audits to maintain compliance.
How do I request a quote?
Upload your CAD files in STEP, IGES, or DXF format through our online quote portal. Our engineering team reviews your design and provides a detailed quote within 24 hours including material costs, machining time, and delivery schedule.
