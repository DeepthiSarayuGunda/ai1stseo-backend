About Bright Smile Dental Clinic

Bright Smile Dental Clinic is a full-service dental practice located at 245 Bank Street in downtown Ottawa. The clinic was founded in 2015 by Dr. Sarah Chen, a graduate of the University of Toronto Faculty of Dentistry. Our team of 4 dentists and 6 hygienists serves over 2,500 patients annually.

We are committed to providing evidence-based dental care using modern technology. Our clinic features digital X-rays that reduce radiation exposure by 80%, intraoral cameras for real-time diagnosis, and a dedicated pediatric treatment room. According to a 2024 survey by the Ontario Dental Association, clinics using digital imaging detect 23% more early-stage cavities than those using traditional film.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the most common questions our patients ask. If you have additional questions, please call us at (613) 555-0199 or visit our contact page.

What services does Bright Smile Dental offer?
We offer general dentistry including cleanings, fillings, and extractions. We also provide cosmetic dentistry such as whitening and veneers, emergency dental care, pediatric dentistry, Invisalign, and dental implant consultations.
Does Bright Smile Dental accept insurance?
Yes, we accept all major dental insurance plans in Ontario. We also offer direct billing to most providers so you pay only your portion at the time of your visit.
How often should I visit the dentist?
The Canadian Dental Association recommends visiting your dentist every 6 months for a routine checkup and cleaning. Patients with gum disease or other conditions may need more frequent visits.
What should I do in a dental emergency?
Call our emergency line at (613) 555-0199 immediately. For a knocked-out tooth, keep it moist in milk and come in within 30 minutes. For severe pain, rinse with warm salt water and apply a cold compress.
How much does a dental cleaning cost without insurance?
A standard dental cleaning at our clinic costs between $150 and $250 depending on the complexity. We offer a 15% discount for patients without insurance who pay at the time of service.
