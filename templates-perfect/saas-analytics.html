What is DataPulse Analytics

DataPulse Analytics is defined as a cloud-based business intelligence platform that uses artificial intelligence to transform raw data into visual dashboards and predictive insights. The platform connects to over 150 data sources and automatically detects patterns, anomalies, and trends that would take analysts hours to find manually.

Founded in 2022, DataPulse serves over 1,200 companies across 40 countries.
