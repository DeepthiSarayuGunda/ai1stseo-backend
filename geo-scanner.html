📖 How to read this report
🎯
Brand Visibility — Did AI models mention your brand when users searched for your industry keywords? Higher % = more AI engines recommend you.

Content Readiness — Is your website structured so AI engines can easily read and cite it? Checks for FAQ schema, headings, and structured data.
