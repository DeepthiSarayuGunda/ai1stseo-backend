## AEO / GEO / AI SEO — What It Means

**AEO (Answer Engine Optimization):**
Optimizing your content so AI-powered answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) can find, understand, and cite your website. Think: structured data, FAQ sections, clear answers, authority signals.

**GEO (Generative Engine Optimization):**
Tracking and improving how often generative AI models mention your brand when users ask relevant questions. Your "GEO Score" = the percentage of AI queries where your brand gets cited.

**Why this matters:**
More people are searching inside AI tools instead of Google. If your brand isn't optimized for AI discovery, you're invisible to a growing audience. AI1stSEO helps you measure and fix that.

## Things to Avoid in Posts

- Don't mention backend tech (AWS, Lambda, Bedrock, PostgreSQL, etc.)
- Don't reference specific code, APIs, or developer dashboards
- Don't claim exact check counts unless rounding ("200+ checks" is fine, avoid "35 technical + 25 on-page...")
- Don't invent case studies or customer testimonials — none exist yet
- Don't claim WCAG compliance or certifications that haven't been verified
- Don't reference competitor pricing unless publicly sourced
- Don't use "best" or "only" without qualification
